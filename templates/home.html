SCOPE

Pixora software is a photography and videography service platform designed to connect clients with professional service providers. It aims to offer an assembled and convenient space where people, companies, and event planners can browse, reserve, and oversee photography services easily. The foremost benefits of this system are time savings through faster booking, greater reach for service providers, and improved convenience for clients. The goals are to build an efficient platform that enables efficient communication, secure transactions, and reliable service delivery.

FOR WHO?

Pixora is developed for people who want to capture their precious moments in a way that transcends natural human capturing capabilities. It is a standalone proprietary web-based application with no dependencies on other software. It is available for anyone willing to spend a small fortune to capture something upon which one can reminisce. Pixora is a user-friendly platform built by software experts on which multiple professional vendors offer their services at reasonable rates with a complete money-back guarantee and the option to cancel a reservation at any time.

FEATURES

  • The system will be able to handle multiple concurrent users.
  • Fast booking and confirmation.
  • Great quality offered by the vendors.
  • Money back guarantee.
  • Offeres people the opportunity to freelance and showcase their talent.
  • Diligent and effective moderation of the platform incase of any scams.
